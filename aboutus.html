The Heart of Ashlaloops

Meet the Maker

"In every stitch, there is a piece of my heart and a lesson in patience."

Hi, I’m Angelica—the hands and heart behind every piece. What started as a simple hobby has grown into something I truly love, and I’m so grateful to share it with you.

How it started

It all began during the COVID-19 pandemic, when life slowed down and I found myself searching for something to fill my time. I came across crochet online and decided to give it a try, thinking it would simply be a small pastime. But from the moment I picked up the yarn and hook, it felt like love at first try.

I’ve always been someone who easily gives up when things don’t go my way. But crochet was different. It didn’t come perfectly at first—I had to undo stitches, start over, and learn things the hard way. Yet somehow, I kept going. Along the way, it taught me patience, to trust the process, and to appreciate the beauty in slow, steady progress.

I started by making pieces for myself, then for my family and friends, pouring care into each one. Over time, that feeling grew into something more—a quiet thought of “I want to share this with other people”. What began as a simple hobby slowly turned into something meaningful. Today, each piece is thoughtfully handmade, carrying a story of growth, care, and passion.

Why Crochet?

One of a Kind

Each stitch is carefully crafted by hand, making every item unique. It allows for full creativity in colors, patterns, and sizes tailored just for you.

Built to Last

Handmade pieces are more durable because the maker controls the tension and quality of every loop, ensuring a piece that stands the test of time.

Slow Fashion

By choosing crochet, you support sustainable, mindful production that preserves traditional craftsmanship over mass manufacturing.

"Most importantly, handmade crochet preserves traditional craftsmanship while adding personal value and meaning to every single piece."
